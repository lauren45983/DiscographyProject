A little bit of a background...

BTS is a 7 member, all male k-pop group. Their music includes a mix of vocals and rap at various tempos, conveying various moods. The majority of their lyrics are in Korean. BTS stands for Bangtan Sonyeondan, which means Bullet-Proof Boyscouts in Korean. They debuted in 2013 under the entertainment company Bighit. BTS started out with a very intense concept, which has softened a bit in recent years.
